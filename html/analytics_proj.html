LAFD Fire Response Analysis

I was looking to ways to use data analytics to help prevent the seasonal fires in southern california, and help better the response of the fire departments.

Upon coming across ~5M+ rows of data, I decided to come up & feature engineer metrics based on which I could evaluate the performance of the fire departments.
After using Pandas & Python to come up with metrics like ResponseTime & DispatchTime, I determined, on average, which fire departments took the longest to dispatch and which ones were burdened,
After identifying the Top 5 most affected site, I was able to suggest at 17% increase in physical resources like FireTrucks & Dispatch Vehicles to the affected sites.
Soccer Analysis
Due to my ever lasting love for soccer and the increasingly important role of analytics in the soccer industry, I decided to analyze the statistics of popular teams and players.
Although my goal was to analyze statistics like interceptions, through balls etc., I also wanted to visualize them and present my findings in a way that could be understood by the lay man.
The data was rarely present in a clean format; I scraped data through tools like Selenium. To present the data I used a mix of Tableau & Python. I posted a writeup on twitter along with the visuals to generate discussion and gather critical feedback.
